Huawei Watch / HarmonyOS

Padel Score

Keep score from your wrist.

Padel Score is a minimal app for keeping padel and tennis score from a Huawei Watch. It works offline, saves matches locally, and keeps match controls available during play.

Features

Core functions for tracking a match from the watch without account setup or network access.

1

One-tap scoring

Tap the team that wins the point and keep the marker moving without phone interaction.

A

Auto-save & resume

Matches are saved locally so you can pause and resume without rebuilding the score.

R

Padel/Tennis rules

Best of 3 or best of 5, Advantage, Golden Point, Win by 2, and tie-break at 6-6.

H

Local match history

Review recent matches stored on the watch, without accounts or cloud sync.

U

Undo / pause / finish

Correct a point, pause a match, or finish cleanly from dedicated watch controls.

O

Offline by design

Designed to work on court without network access, login, or external services.

How it works

A short flow for starting a match from the watch.

1

Install on Huawei Watch

Use the compatible Huawei Watch / HarmonyOS installation path for your region.

2

Open Padel Score

Launch the app directly from the watch.

3

Set match format

Choose best of 3 or best of 5 and the scoring rule for the match.

4

Choose who serves first

Set the first server and follow the serve indicator during play.

5

Tap the team that wins the point

The score updates from the wrist, including tie-breaks at 6-6.
