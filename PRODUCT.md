# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Menvic clients, internal collaborators, Revit users, app reviewers, and support visitors who need a clear public entry point for Menvic tools. They arrive looking for Revit automation resources, app information, privacy policies, support links, or learning material.

## Product Purpose

pyMenvic is the public technical hub for Menvic apps, pyMenvic Revit tools, support pages, and learning resources. Success means visitors can quickly understand what Menvic publishes, find the correct route, and trust that support and policy pages are maintained.

## Brand Personality

Calm, technical, precise.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS marketing pages, AI-purple gradient heroes, oversized decorative cards, emoji-heavy app directories, dark pages that sacrifice readability, and noisy promotional copy.

## Design Principles

- Make navigation the product: routes and support links should be obvious.
- Keep the tone practical: describe tools by what they do and where they run.
- Treat Revit utilities as the technical core while giving apps a clean public home.
- Prefer quiet hierarchy, strong contrast, and restrained surfaces over decorative effects.
- Preserve public policy and support routes exactly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, reduced-motion support, semantic landmarks, and short readable line lengths.
